Lionel Andrés Messi

  • 24 June 1987 - born in Rosario, Santa Fe.
  • 1992 - He joined a local club, Grandoli, where he was coached by his father.
  • 2003 - After being named player of the tournament in four international pre-season competitions with the Juvenile B, he played only one official match with the team before being promoted to the Juveniles A.
  • 2004 - His progress was reflected in his first contract signed on 4 February, which lasted until 2012.
  • 2005 - On 24 June, his 18th birthday, Messi signed his first contract as a senior player.
  • 2005 - Messi established himself as one of the best players in the world, scoring 17 goals in 36 games across all competitions.
  • 2008 - Upon Rijkaard and Ronaldinho's departure, Messin was given the number 10 shirt. He signed a new contract in July with an annual salary of €7.8 million, becoming the highest paid player.
  • 2014 - Under new manager and former captain Luis Enrique, Messi experienced a largely injury-free start to the 2014 season allowing him to break three more longstanding records towards the end of the year.
  • 2020 - On 23 December, Messi scored his 644th goal for Barcelona against Real Valladolid in La Liga, surpassing Pelè with Santos as the player with the most goals scored for a single club.
  • 2021 - On 10 August, Messi joined French club Paris Saint-Germain, signing a two-year contract. He chose 30 as his squad number, the same he wore as a teenager when he made his senior debut for Barcelona.
